Business Model for Caf.js

  • Framework, and basic self-hosting, always free and open source
  • Caf.js Cloud revenue a multiple of infrastructure costs
  • Not a Nanny Store
    • No pre-approval for publishing
    • Manage your own margins, or in-app purchases
  • Cheap hosting prices enabled by custom platform
    • Baseline: 10 cents per week for an app in incubator mode
    • Gold plan: $0.85 per CA (Cloud Assistant) per year
      • A DIY with Google serverless is $144 per year
  • One billion CAs with a few thousand large VMs
  • Bootstrap apps with no money or credit card
